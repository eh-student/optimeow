Frequently Asked Questions About Grain-Free Cat Food

Common questions about our premium grain-free chicken cat food and high protein adult cat food formula.

Is grain-free cat food better for my adult cat?

Grain-free cat food can be beneficial for cats with grain sensitivities or allergies. Our grain-free chicken cat food is easier to digest and provides high-quality protein from real chicken as the first ingredient, making it an excellent choice for adult cats.

What makes this high protein cat food special?

Our grain-free chicken cat food contains 38% protein, making it ideal for maintaining lean muscle mass in adult cats. The formula includes real deboned chicken as the first ingredient, providing complete amino acid profiles essential for feline health.

How do I transition my cat to this grain-free cat food?

Gradually transition your cat over 7-10 days by mixing increasing amounts of our grain-free chicken cat food with decreasing amounts of their current food. This helps prevent digestive upset and allows your cat to adjust to the new high protein cat food formula.
